Welcome to the OverWatcher!
This tool allows you to view EOS Mainnet Transactions for most of the popular coins.
Buys are Green, Sells are Red, Newdex orders are Purple, Lending actions are yellow/orange and arbitrage bots are Gray (you can disable them from the settings).
For any issues, questions or bug reports please join our Telegram Channel and let us know how we can improve it.
